Our Story

Founded on plain-language advice.

Founded in 2012, Aspire Immigration Services has grown from its Surrey head office to branches in Toronto, Edmonton, and Chandigarh, India, while keeping every file under the direct supervision of a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant.

We work with study-abroad aspirants, skilled workers, business persons, and investors, and monitor our operational capacity closely so each client gets the attention their file needs.
