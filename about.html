Our Story

Founded on plain-language advice.

Harbourline Immigration opened its first office in Surrey in 2013. Since then, the firm has grown to three Canadian offices while keeping every file under the direct supervision of a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant — never handed off to unsupervised staff.

We work with individuals, families, and a small number of employer partners, and we turn away files we don't believe have a realistic chance of success, rather than take the fee anyway.
