Every pathway, handled properly.

From your first study permit to sponsoring family members years later, each service below is led by a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant — not a junior assistant working from a script.

FILE 01

Express Entry & Permanent Residence

We calculate your Comprehensive Ranking System score, identify ways to strengthen it, and manage your profile through to an Invitation to Apply and final PR submission.

  • Federal Skilled Worker, Canadian Experience Class, and Federal Skilled Trades eligibility review
  • CRS score optimization, including language retesting and credential assessment guidance
  • Full document package preparation for the final e-APR submission

Best for: Skilled workers with Canadian or foreign work experience who want to apply for permanent residence directly.

Typical timeline: 6–14 months, depending on draw frequency and CRS score.

FILE 02

Study Permits

We help you select a Designated Learning Institution, prepare a genuine study plan, and compile the financial and academic evidence IRCC expects to see.

  • Letter of acceptance and DLI verification review
  • Proof-of-funds structuring and study plan drafting support
  • Guidance on study permit extensions and co-op work permits

Best for: Prospective and current international students, including extensions and program changes.

Typical timeline: 4–12 weeks depending on country of application.

FILE 03

Work Permits

Whether your job offer requires an LMIA or falls under an exemption category, we prepare the supporting documentation employers and officers expect.

  • LMIA-based and LMIA-exempt work permit applications (CUSMA, intra-company transfer, and more)
  • Post-Graduation Work Permit applications for recent graduates
  • Employer-side guidance on compliance and offer of employment submissions

Best for: Skilled workers with a Canadian job offer, and employers looking to hire from abroad.

Typical timeline: 8–16 weeks, longer where an LMIA is required.

FILE 04

Family & Spousal Sponsorship

We build relationship evidence packages that hold up to scrutiny and guide sponsors through their own eligibility requirements before filing begins.

  • Spousal and common-law partner sponsorship, inland and outland
  • Dependent children and parent/grandparent sponsorship applications
  • Support responding to procedural fairness letters and interview preparation

Best for: Canadian citizens and permanent residents sponsoring family members.

Typical timeline: 10–24 months depending on relationship category.

FILE 05

Visitor Visas

We prepare temporary resident visa and eTA applications with the ties-to-home-country evidence officers look for, and can help respond after a prior refusal.

  • Tourist, family visit, and business travel visa applications
  • Super Visa applications for parents and grandparents
  • Refusal review and reapplication strategy

Best for: Travellers visiting Canada for tourism, family, or business purposes.

Typical timeline: 2–8 weeks depending on visa office.

FILE 06

Provincial Nominee Programs

Every province runs different streams with different criteria. We match your profile to the strongest available stream and manage the nomination and PR stages.

  • Stream eligibility review across BC, Ontario, and other participating provinces
  • Expression of interest and nomination application drafting
  • Coordination with your Express Entry profile where streams are aligned

Best for: Applicants with skills or job offers tied to a specific province.

Typical timeline: Varies by province and stream, typically 4–12 months to nomination.
